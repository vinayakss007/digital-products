## Full Description

### Why This CRM?
Most freelancers and small business owners track leads in a messy notebook, WhatsApp chats, or spreadsheets with broken formulas. You lose deals because you forget to follow up. You can't see which stage your pipeline is at. You don't know your conversion rate.

This template fixes that.

### What You Get
✅ **Lead database** — track company, contact, deal value, source, stage, tags
✅ **Pipeline stages** — Cold → Contacted → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed
✅ **Auto follow-up reminders** — daily email alerts so you never miss a follow-up
✅ **Revenue dashboard** — see pipeline value, avg deal size, conversion rate at a glance
✅ **Activity log** — every interaction logged with timestamps
✅ **Probability-weighted pipeline** — know your real expected revenue
✅ **One-click stage updates** — drag leads through your pipeline from a custom menu

### Built For
- Solo founders bootstrapping their business
- Freelancers managing their own sales pipeline
- Small teams (2-10 people) without a CRM budget
- Consultants tracking multiple leads simultaneously

### How It Works
1. Create a copy in Google Sheets
2. Paste the script into Apps Script (5 clicks, copy-paste)
3. Set up your daily email reminder trigger (1 click)
4. Start adding leads

Everything runs in Google Sheets — free, on any device, accessible anywhere.
